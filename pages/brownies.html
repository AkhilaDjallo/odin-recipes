Brownie Recipe

Description

These rich and decadent brownies are the ultimate chocolate lover's dessert. Made with simple ingredients and easy to follow steps, this recipe is a guaranteed crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
