Spaghetti Recipe

Description

This classic spaghetti recipe is simple, delicious, and perfect for a quick and easy weeknight dinner. With just a few basic ingredients, you can create a comforting and satisfying meal that the whole family will love.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 4 quarts water
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. In a large pot, bring the water to a rolling boil. Add the salt and spaghetti, and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dante.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and red pepper flakes (if using), and cook for 1-2 minutes or until fragrant.
  3. Drain the spaghetti, reserving 1 cup of the pasta water. Add the spaghetti to the skillet with the garlic and oil, and toss to coat. If the pasta seems dry, add some of the reserved pasta water a little at a time until the desired consistency is reached.
  4. Remove the skillet from the heat, and add the Parmesan cheese, tossing again to combine. Serve hot and enjoy!
